About Us

Company Information: Background on Pharmainsight and its founder, Michael Kutuzov.

We want to offer the pharmaceutical market a new service – finding drugs in the pharmacy nearby. The pain point of my customers is that not all the prescribed or needed drugs can be found in any pharmacy. Many customers must go through long trips searching for the medicines they need. It is frustrating when doctors prescribe a long list of drugs and people spend long time collecting in different pharmacies in the city. In our company, we facilitate trade between the pharmacy stores and buyers.
